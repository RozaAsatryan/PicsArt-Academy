ABOUT US

Our History

We founded Paper Mania in 2014. One day we realized that we didn't want to wrap gifts for our loved ones in low-quality, old-fashioned bags. That's when the idea came to mind to create our own. Originally a small workshop, where we created Kraft bags for gifts and souvenirs, in subsequent years we have developed into a company that produces not only bags, but also wrapping paper, boxes, paper accessories for packaging.

Our task

We want the process of giving gifts to bring as much pleasure and joy as receiving them.
